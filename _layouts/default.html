Research

Currently I'm interested in Fine/Dexterous Manipulation and Mobile Manipulation, and which approach, sim2real or demonstrations would be the ultimate solution for Generalist Robots 🤖. Towards this goal, I think it's crucial that we learn how to better leverage familiar modalities like vision 👀 or language 💬, while touching other important yet underexplored modalities like tactile 👉🏻👈🏻 or sound 👂🏻.
